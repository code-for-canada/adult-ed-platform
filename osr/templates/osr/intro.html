{% blocktrans %}Adult learning{% endblocktrans %}

Adult learning offers programs for those who want to upgrade their skills, get their high school diploma, earn credits and/or improve their language skills.

Upgrade your skills to get better job opportunities, develop confidence and pursue further education

Get your Ontario high school diploma and/or earn the marks needed for post-secondary education

{% blocktrans %}Who can benefit from adult learning programs?{% endblocktrans %}

  • Adult residents of Ontario (at least 18 or 19 years of age, depending on the program).
  • Those who want to improve their skills towards education, apprenticeship, employment and/or self-confidence.
  • Those who want to get an Ontario high school diploma and/or earn credits for post-secondary education.
  • Immigrants who want to improve their language skills in English or French.
  • Highly skilled immigrants who want to continue working in their fields.
